How do I follow a post?

Click the star in the upper right hand corner to add a post to your followed posts list. You can find the posts you are following by going to "Posts I'm Following" under the discussion categories tab.

Can I delete or modify my posts?

No. However, you may reply to your own post with any corrections you wish to make.

How do I view the discussion in progress in the courseware?

Click the "Show discussion" icon.

How do I search the discussion?

Click the magifying glass icon.

How do I find my own posts or another users posts?

Click the magifying glass icon and enter the username whose posts you wish to find (note, the search is case sensitive).

How do I add an image to my discussion post?

When creating your post, click the square, green and blue icon that appears above the text box.

Where are my scores for the entrance survey?

You are not graded on the entrance survey. Once completed your scores will be appear as 0/0.

Problem Scores versus Practice Scores?

Graded sections have problem scores, while ungraded sections have practice scores.

When are assignments / tests / other graded items due?

The assignment deadlines appear in Coordinated 24 hour Universal Time (UTC). We advise students to submit their assignments a minimum of 5 minutes before the deadline to avoid synchronization issues. Click here to see the course syllabus for further details regarding specific deadlines.

Do I have to complete all the course content?

You are free to explore as much or as little of the course content as you like. However, if you wish to attain the certificate of completion it is advisable that you complete the graded content. See the course Syllabus for further details

I cannot submit my assignment / test / other graded item?

Please check the due date. If the assignment due date has passed you will be unable to submit your assignment or test answers.

I started the course late, can I get a deadline extension on the graded content?

Unfortunately, due to the volume of participants in the course we cannot make considerations on a case by case basis.

When do I get my grade?

Your grades will be calculated and displayed in the progress bar instantaneously. If you achieved the requisite final course grade you will receive your Honour Code Attestation after the course ends.
